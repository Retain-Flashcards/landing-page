Retain is a flashcard app designed to help you master large volumes of content quickly. Built by a medical student, its spaced repetition algorithm, intuitive interface, and seamless cloud syncing makes it a powerful tool to accelerate your learning.

Design That Doesn't Drain You.

Students in high-intensity programs like medical school often review flashcards for hours on end. Why not have a user interface that feels enjoyable to use? Retain replaces outdated, visually exhausting interfaces with a modern, vibrant design. Customize the colors for each deck, giving every subject a unique visual identity that reduces cognitive fatigue.

Keep Your Decks. Upgrade Your Experience.

Want to bring your Anki flashcards to Retain? We've got you covered.

Our import utility analyzes your deck and lets you import by tags. With popular decks like the AnKing Step deck, you can import the content you need for each topic as you go!

Card Creation Made Simple

  • Clean Cloze Deletions

    No more confusing {{c1::bracket}} syntax. Highlight and hide text with a simple button or keyboard shortcut.

  • Side-by-Side Notes

    Upload PDFs directly into the web editor to view notes alongside your cards.

  • Drag, Clip, Paste

    Spot a vital diagram? Drag to clip sections of your notes and instantly paste them into your flashcards.

AI-Powered

Your Personal AI Study Partner.

Retain leverages AI to deliver study tools legacy apps simply cannot match. Use the hands-free voice study mode to review cards while walking or cooking; the AI quizzes you aloud and grades your responses in the background.

Type a focus like "cardiovascular pharmacology," and natural language sorting curates an instant session. When clipping images from notes, Retain’s AI even analyzes the context and suggests flashcard content to save you hours of typing.

Study Anywhere, Anytime. Literally.

Retain logs your reviews instantly in the cloud, eliminating manual syncing and letting you seamlessly switch from your laptop to your phone. Our advanced spaced repetition algorithm ensures maximum long-term retention.

Deck Collaboration

Share custom decks with classmates and grant collaborative editor access - build better study guides together!
